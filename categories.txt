# Email Classification Categories
# Format: CategoryName: Description
#
# Edit this file to customize categories. The LLM will use these
# descriptions to classify emails into the appropriate category.

Financial: Investment and brokerage communications ONLY. Includes trade
confirmations, tax documents (1099s, W-2s), dividend notices, portfolio reviews,
and financial advisor newsletters from brokerages (Schwab, Fidelity, Vanguard).
Does NOT include payment receipts, billing confirmations, or donation receipts -
those belong in Receipts. Does NOT include credit card statements or bank
transaction alerts - those are AccountSecurity or Receipts.

OnlineServices: Digital subscriptions and online service billing. Includes
hosting invoices (DigitalOcean, AWS, Linode), domain renewals (GoDaddy,
Namecheap), software subscriptions (Microsoft 365, Adobe, JetBrains),
streaming services (Netflix, Spotify), and cloud platform billing.

Receipts: Payment receipts, invoices, and payment confirmations for ANY purchase
or payment. Includes retail receipts, PayPal/Venmo transactions, vendor invoices,
refund confirmations, donation receipts (ActBlue, charities, nonprofits),
healthcare payment confirmations, automatic payment notifications, cloud service
payments (Google Cloud, AWS), and any "thank you for your payment" emails. If it
confirms a payment was made, it's a Receipt.

AccountSecurity: Security alerts, account access, and verification notifications.
Includes password reset requests, two-factor authentication codes, login alerts,
suspicious activity warnings, email verification requests, "confirm your email"
messages, account activation links, and verification codes from new signups.

Orders: Order confirmations and shipping notifications. Includes purchase
confirmations, shipping updates, delivery notifications, tracking information,
and order status updates from online and retail purchases.

Shopping: Automated shopping communications FROM RETAILERS. Includes cart
reminders, wishlist updates, back-in-stock alerts, price drop notifications,
and browsing-related emails from online stores (Amazon, eBay, etc). Does NOT
include personal emails where friends discuss products or purchases casually.

Promotions: Marketing promotions, deals, and coupons. Includes discount offers,
sale announcements, promotional codes, special offers, loyalty rewards, and
advertising emails.

Events: Event invitations and notifications. Includes party invites, meeting
requests, calendar invitations, event reminders, RSVPs, webinar invitations,
and updates about scheduled events.

Personal: Personal communications from friends and family. This category takes
PRIORITY over topic-based categories when the sender is a person (not a business
or automated system). Includes personal emails, casual conversations, family
updates, discussions about any topic between friends, and non-automated messages
from individual email addresses. If an email is from a person discussing
repairs, shopping, travel, or any other topic casually, classify as Personal.

SocialMedia: Social media and community notifications. Includes Facebook,
Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn notifications, forum updates, group messages,
friend requests, likes, comments, and community digest emails.

Healthcare: Medical and health-related communications. Includes appointment
reminders, patient portal messages, test results, prescription notifications,
health insurance, fitness class bookings, and wellness program updates.

Career: Work and career-related communications. Includes job alerts, recruiter
messages, professional development, LinkedIn job notifications, resume updates,
and career opportunity emails.

ProductUpdates: Product announcements and release notifications. Includes
software updates, new feature announcements, product launches, changelog
notifications, and technology news from companies and services you use.

TechSupport: Technical support and help desk communications. Includes support
ticket updates, troubleshooting responses, bug reports, feature requests, and
customer service correspondence for technical issues.

Newsletters: Newsletters, news digests, and editorial content. Includes email
newsletters, news roundups, blog digests, article recommendations, and
subscription-based informational content.

FeedbackRequests: Surveys and feedback requests. Includes customer satisfaction
surveys, product review requests, NPS surveys, feedback forms, and rating
requests.

Travel: Travel and transportation communications. Includes flight confirmations,
hotel bookings, rental car reservations, travel itineraries, boarding passes,
trip updates, and transportation service notifications.

HomeImprovement: Home improvement and maintenance communications FROM BUSINESSES.
Includes contractor quotes, home service appointments, hardware store emails
(Home Depot, Lowes, Ace), plumber/electrician/HVAC service communications, and
home warranty providers. Does NOT include personal emails discussing home topics.
