# Email Classification Categories
# Format: CategoryName: Description
#
# Edit this file to customize categories. The LLM will use these
# descriptions to classify emails into the appropriate category.

Financial: Communications from banks, brokerages, and financial institutions
about your accounts, investments, loans, or tax documents.

OnlineServices: Communications from online services and platforms you use about
billing, account status, policy changes, or feature updates.

Receipts: Confirmations that a payment was made. Includes purchase receipts,
donation acknowledgments, and payment confirmations.

AccountSecurity: Security and authentication messages. Password resets,
verification codes, login alerts, and suspicious activity warnings.

Orders: Order lifecycle communications. Purchase confirmations, shipping
updates, delivery notifications, and tracking information.

Shopping: Retailers trying to sell you products. Cart reminders, product
announcements, back-in-stock alerts, and personalized recommendations.

Promotions: Marketing messages. Discount offers, sales, coupons, loyalty
programs, and surveys requesting feedback.

Events: Invitations and updates about scheduled events. Party invites,
meeting requests, calendar items, and event reminders.

Personal: Direct communications from individuals. Emails where the sender is a
person (not a company or automated system) discussing any topic. This category
takes PRIORITY over topic-based categories for person-to-person messages.

SocialMedia: Notifications from social media platforms about activity on your
accounts. Likes, comments, friend requests, and messages from the platform.

Healthcare: Medical and health-related communications. Appointments, test
results, prescriptions, patient portal messages, and wellness programs.

Career: Job and career-related messages. Job alerts, recruiter outreach,
interview requests, and professional development.

TechSupport: Customer support communications. Support tickets, troubleshooting
responses, and help desk correspondence.

Newsletters: Editorial content you subscribe to. News digests, blog posts,
articles, and informational updates from publications or creators.

Travel: Trip-related communications. Flight confirmations, hotel bookings,
itineraries, boarding passes, and transportation updates.
